About Us



Our Vision

To be the leading secondary education provider in Sri Lanka


Established in year 2018, "Akura" Educational Institute is a leading secondary education provider in Colombo 05, Sri Lanka with a larger number of sudents in education industry.

The institute comprises of a strong panel of instructors who are academically and professionally qualified. The teaching and learning environment of us includes modern and structured coaching methods designed for effective learning.

Institute is mainly offering complete theory notes including necessary practical notes and newly prepared model papers and past papers with answers. Students can get download required learning materials through this.

Institute is providing learning materials in all three medium; Sinhala, English and Tamil for the betterment. The students are provided free guidance and counselling services to face the examinations well and obtain the admission to top universities in Sri Lanka.
